About KalaSetu

Bridging Tradition with Tomorrow

Our Story

KalaSetu was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a bridge (Setu) between India's rich artistic heritage (Kala) and the modern world. We witnessed talented artisans struggling to reach wider markets while their centuries-old crafts faced the risk of extinction.

Founded in 2024, we set out to change this narrative. Our platform connects traditional Indian artisans directly with art enthusiasts and collectors worldwide, ensuring that every purchase supports the livelihood of skilled craftspeople and helps preserve India's cultural legacy.

Today, KalaSetu proudly serves as home to thousands of artisans from across India, from the pottery workshops of West Bengal to the textile looms of Gujarat, from the painting studios of Bihar to the jewelry makers of Rajasthan.
