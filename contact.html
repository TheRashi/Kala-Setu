Frequently Asked Questions

For Buyers

How do I place an order? +
Browse our shop, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in to complete your purchase.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
How long does shipping take? +
Delivery typically takes 5-7 business days within India. International shipping may take 10-15 business days depending on the destination.
What is your return policy? +
We offer a 7-day return policy for damaged or defective items. Handmade items may have slight variations which are not considered defects.

For Sellers

How do I become a seller? +
Visit our Sell page, fill out the registration form with your details and artwork information. Our team will verify your submission and get back to you within 2-3 business days.
What commission does KalaSetu charge? +
We charge a competitive commission of 15-20% on each sale, which covers platform maintenance, payment processing, and marketing support.
When do I receive payment? +
Payments are processed within 7 days after the product is delivered to the customer. Funds are transferred directly to your registered bank account.
Do you provide shipping support? +
Yes! We provide packaging guidelines and partner with reliable courier services. We can also arrange pickup from your location.
